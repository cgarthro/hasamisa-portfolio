The Vision

The Casberry AI Particle Simulator was developed as a high-fidelity visualization tool for complex atomic and neural patterns. By leveraging GPU compute shaders, we achieved a stable simulation of over 20,000 active units moving in harmonious geometric formations.

20K+ Units

Technical Deep Dive

The core of the simulator uses a Geodesic Sphere distribution algorithm, ensuring perfectly uniform point density across spherical surfaces. The "Smart Text Engine" allows users to morph particle clouds into high-contrast typography, bridging the gap between abstract art and functional UI.

Final Outcome

A premium, interactive experience that showcases the power of browser-based 3D computing. Casberry represents the intersection of technical excellence and luxurious design.
